Serve Humanity Welfare

Serve Humanity is a non-profitable welfare organization dedicated to improving the lives of needy and poor people.

Established on 13-05-2023, the organization has resolved more than 100 cases so far. We collect donations as well as cases from all over Pakistan. Serve Humanity collaborates with the Pakistan Citizen Alliance to run a highly successful annual winter drive. Our members and volunteers are key contributors to our impact, and we recognize their hard work with certifications like Appreciation, Achievement, and Donor of the Month awards.
